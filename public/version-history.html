v0.9.1-beta ACGCN item icons 1 day after v0.9.0
  • Animal Crossing (GameCube) item icons across fish, bugs, fossils, and art — 118 items, sourced from the AC Fandom Wiki under CC BY-SA 3.0
  • Icons appear across category rows, expand panels, global search results, and home tab strips
  • Future games light up automatically when their icon assets land — no code change required
  • New /credits route documenting third-party asset sources and licensing
  • Other games (ACWW, ACCF, ACNL, ACNH) keep the existing monogram fallback until their icon sets ship

  • Unified TownManager drawer for create, switch, edit, and delete
  • Settings page with About + Danger zone (reset active town's donations, reset everything)
  • HomeTab rebuilt — hero stat, month strip, leaving-soon and just-arrived shelves, segmented progress meter
  • CategoryTab sectioned into Leaving / Available / Out of season / Already donated
  • Global search dropdown with category groups, keyboard navigation, and search history
  • StatsTab redesigned — per-category cards and a yearly rhythm chart
  • Art tab now uses the same inline expand panel as every other category
  • Wild World + City Folk art data added; full mobile responsive pass
